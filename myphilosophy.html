My Philosophy

Why I'm Doing What I'm Doing

As many know, living with a mental illness is not easy and it is especially lonely due to the stigma surrounding it. In my early teens I struggled a lot with my mental health and looking back at my experiences the only part that I wish had been different was having someone that I could talk to about what I was going through. ​

I created this website because I want to help others who feel the same way I did, to me having someone to talk to would have made dealing with my mental health struggles less lonely. I also would like to raise awareness about the use of support groups that assist with mental health. It is a common universal thought that the only way to support someone dealing with a mental illness is by seeking professional help. This may be true in some aspects; however, the use of support groups and discussion circles can help those who are not ready to seek out professional help by allowing them to express their feelings and to know that they are not alone in their battles.

With that being said, welcome to my circle, feel free to stay a while ♥
